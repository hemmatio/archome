Our services

Archome Improvements is your comprehensive solution for all home renovation needs. As a private contracting company, we specialize in a wide array of services designed to transform your living spaces. Our expertise extends to kitchen and bathroom renovations, where we bring modern designs and high-quality materials together to create spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Our basement finishing services turn underutilized areas into comfortable and usable spaces, tailored to your specific needs.

Our flooring specialists are adept at installing and finishing a variety of floor types, including hardwood and tile, ensuring both durability and visual appeal. We also offer drywalling and plastering services, providing smooth and flawless walls ready for painting. Our professional painters then bring color and life to your rooms, with meticulous attention to detail and quality.

Outside, our landscaping services transform your outdoor spaces into beautiful and enjoyable extensions of your home. We design and build decks and fences, install patio stones, and create landscapes that are both beautiful and easy to maintain.

In addition, our refurnishing services can rejuvenate your existing furniture and decor, giving them a fresh and updated look.

At Archome Improvements, our commitment to quality, excellence, and customer satisfaction is at the heart of every service we offer. We are your go-to choice for all home renovation needs.
